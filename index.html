Yokohama City

Yokohama is the second largest city in Japan by population, and the most populous minicipality of Japan.It is the capital city of Kanagawa Prefecture. It lies on Tokyo Bay, south of Tokyo, in the Kant≈ç region of the main island of Honshu. It is a major commercial hub of the Greater Tokyo Area. Yokohama developed rapidly as Japan's prominent port city following the end of Japan's relative isolation in the mid-19th century, and is today one of its major ports along with Kobe, Osaka, Nagoya, Hakata, Tokyo, and Chiba.

There are number of places that are famous for sightseeing.Some of the top sights include:

  1. Yokohama Chinatown
  2. Enoshima
  3. Yokohama Landmark Tower
  4. Sankeien Garden
  5. Yokohama Hakkeijima Sea Paradise
  6. Osanbashi Pier Building
  7. Yamashita Park
  8. Cup Noodles Museum
  9. Shin-Yokohama Ramen Museum
